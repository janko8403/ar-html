AR Bridge. Customize your message.

AR Bridge uses a full potential of Augmented Reality in combination with geolocalization and advanced image recognition algorithms and artificial intelligence. AR Bridge also enables planning, coordinating and managing multiple AR marketing campaigns and channels of communication. It can create a completely new experience for customers.

  • Dedicated AR App
  • AR Module
  • AR Bridge Platform

AR Bridge App is a completely new approach to sales pitches and marketing campaigns. User becomes an integral part of the experience and receives personalized offers and ads. Information can be delivered in an engaging and innovative way.

If you already own an AR mobile app you can easily connect it with our AR Module. It can be connected with an existing app as an additional tab and can use the full potential of AR right away.

AR Bridge is an intuitive platform to manage multiple ad and marketing campaigns in real time. It is a great tool for omni-channel campaigns and allows for a brand new way of communication with customers. It delivers spot-on analytics and user statistics, needed when creating personalized offers.
