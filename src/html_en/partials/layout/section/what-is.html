What is
Augmented Reality?

AR is the integration of digital images with a real world. It connects both worlds on a screen of a mobile device, in real time.

Imagine a world where you can point your device at any given object and immediately receive a full information about it.

AR can completely changes the way we learn, the way we train staff and support field workers. For marketing departments it is a brand new tool which allows users to become a part of a storyline.
